Recruitment Rules

There are few rules before joining IUFC

  1. All potential recruits must demonstrate their gameplay skills through a series of matches in the trial room to ensure they meet our crew's competitive standards.
  2. We value teamwork, camaraderie, and communication. Recruits should show respect to fellow members. No type of abuse, hatred, taunting, teasing will be entertained in our crew.
  3. We expect our members to maintain the best of their behavior. A strong, positive and sportsmanlike attitude, regardless of anything is always expected.
  4. Double crewing is strictly prohibited. If you want to participate in a non-crew tournament take prior permission from the admin panel.
  5. Regular participation is crucial. Gamers need to be active and available for friendly practice, intra-crew tournaments, and other crew events. Inactivity without prior notice/proper reason may result in removal from the crew after a warning.
  6. We seek the highest standard and continuously look for gamers who are committed to improving their skills. Recruits should show a willingness to learn, adapt, and strive for excellence in their gameplay.
  7. No banter is allowed in sensitive topics (e.g., EB-MB, Real-Barca, religion, politics, etc.). We have representatives pan India and Bangladesh and we expect new recuits to maintain the elegance of diversity by all means.

By adhering to these rules, we aim to create a competitive and respectful environment within our eFootball crew. Say cheers to POWER. SPIRIT. GLORY.
